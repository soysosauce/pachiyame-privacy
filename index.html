Privacy Policy - Pachiyame (Quit Pachinko App)

Last updated: March 15, 2026

1. Information We Collect

The Pachiyame app stores the following data locally on your device only. This information is never transmitted to external servers:

  • Date of last pachinko visit (recovery start date)
  • Urge logs (date/time, location, intensity of gambling urges)
  • Emergency contact information (name and phone number)
  • Danger zone location data (latitude/longitude of registered locations)
  • App settings (money saved per session)

2. Location Information

The app uses background location access to detect when you approach danger zones (gambling establishments) that you have manually registered. Location data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to any external server or third party.

3. Phone/Call Feature

The app can open your device's phone dialer to call emergency contacts you have registered. The app itself does not make calls, record calls, or access your contacts list. Phone numbers are stored only on your device.

4. Push Notifications

The app uses local push notifications to alert you when you approach a registered danger zone. These notifications are generated entirely on your device with no external server communication.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer any collected information to third parties under any circumstances.

6. Data Deletion

All data stored by this app can be completely deleted by uninstalling the app from your device.

7. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated without prior notice. Changes take effect when posted on this page.

8. Contact

For any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact us through the app's Google Play Store page.
